About Ashleigh

Hello.

I'm an aspiring freelance Web developer learning front-end web devlopment and maintaining a few WordPress sites for clients. I live in Pennsylvania, outside of Philadelphia but grew up in Vermont. I enjoy my morning cup of coffee, traveling to far off places (but rarely do these days), and dreaming up diy projects inspired by Pinterest.

I have dabbled in coding throughout various parts of my life. However, it wasn't until I was maintaining the WordPress site for my Pilates studio that realized how much I enjoyed working on the web. After selling my pilates studio, I began blogging and found that I enjoyed designing my blog and working on the back-end more than writing content and publishing posts! Like many web developers, I have learned by googling topics, taking classes online through Treehouse and in-person classes through Girl Develop It's Philadelphia chapter, participating in an open-source fellowship, and through the Thinkful front-end mentorship program.
